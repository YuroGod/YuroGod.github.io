解法2 - 表达式化简求解

将那一坨位运算合并一下得到result = ~(data[i] & s[i]) & (data[i] ^ s[i])

所以我们需要在已知data[i]和result的情况下求解s[i]

先将表达式化简，需要一点点逻辑代数的知识

$$ \begin{aligned} result &= \sim(data[i] \& s[i]) \& (data[i] \oplus s[i]) \\ &= (\sim data[i] \mid \sim s[i]) \& (data[i] \oplus s[i]) \\ &= (\sim data[i] \& (data[i] \oplus s[i])) \mid (\sim s[i] \& (data[i] \oplus s[i])) \\ &= (\sim data[i] \& data[i]) \oplus (\sim data[i] \& s[i]) \mid (\sim s[i] \& data[i]) \oplus (\sim s[i] \& s[i]) \\ &= (\sim data[i] \& s[i]) \mid (\sim s[i] \& data[i]) \\ &= data[i] \oplus s[i] \end{aligned} $$

可以得知加密算法其实相当于一个异或而已，直接解就行了
